About this site

Secret of the Wings

In Secret of the Wings, Tinkerbell travels to the Winter Woods. During her trip, Tinkerbell meets a Frost-talent fairy named Periwinkle who was born out of the same baby’s laugh. Because of this, they are considered twin sisters due to the fact that not only do they look alike and think alike , their wings have the exact same pattern on it, a rare feature, since usually every fairy’s wings are unique.
