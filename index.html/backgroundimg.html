Kayin State

Kayin State, formerly known as Karen State, is a state of Myanmar. The capital city is Hpa-An, also spelled Pa-An. The terrain of the state is mountainous; with the Dawna Range running along the state in a NNW-SSE direction, and the southern end of the Karen Hills in the northwest.
